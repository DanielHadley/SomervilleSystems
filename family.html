The City of Somerville's Key Systems Indicators

Housing

Somerville's stock of affordable housing units has increased by nearly 500 since 2002. These units represent almost 10% of the City's housing stock. Somerville residents set a goal of adding an additional 1,200 affordable units through the SomerVision process.

Demographics

Women of reproductive age in Somerville have been more likely to give birth than their counterparts in Massachusetts since 2007.



The proportion of Somerville's population between the ages of 20 and 34 is remarkably large, over 40%, which is more than double the equivalent proportion in Massachusetts. In fact, Somerville has the second largest concentration of 25- to 34-year-olds in the country behind Hoboken, NJ.



Somerville is an incredibly diverse city. Over a quarter of the population was born outside of the United States, and our residents speak more than 35 languages at home.



There is also diversity in family structure in Somerville: the City has the 6th largest concentration of same-sex couple households in the country.
