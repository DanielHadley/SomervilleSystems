Neighborhood Satisfaction

Residents in Davis Square and Union Square rate their neighborhood satisfaction very high. East Somerville is still high, but relatively lower than other parts of the city.

Public Safety

Quality of Life 311 Calls

This is a random sample of the top quality-of-life calls from the year 2013: reports of rats, graffiti and noisy neighbors. It shows where these types of calls tend to cluster, e.g., noise calls in the vicinity of Tufts.

City Operations

Over 80 percent of calls to 311 are answered within 20 seconds. Many of these calls result in the creation of a work order for the Department of Public Works, which completes over 80 percent of its work orders on time. There is variation over the year, though, such as when heavy snow pushed the on-time completion rate in February down to 51 percent.



Fiscal Management

Somerville has some of the lowest property taxes in the metropolitan area when the residential exemption is factored in. The City has had to rely more on taxes as state aid has delined, but still maintains a generous exemption for residents. The largest categories of expenditures are the schools, public safety and works, and the pensions and benefits of employees.
